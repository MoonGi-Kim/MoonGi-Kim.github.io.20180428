Final Project App

Market Go is one of Scavenger hunt type app. Market Go allows users to buy affordable local products through events. The app will connect you to local stores and introduce you to a good neighborhood stores. For example, if you participate in a burger event, you can find good butcher shop, bakery and grocery stores in your area and buy products at event prices. By purchasing the event products registered in the event, users can consume wisely and local shops can increase their sales and promote the shop by registering their store products in the local events. It is smart app for shopping and sales!
